Madeline Wuntch as the Commissioner of the NYPD

Madeline Wuntch

Record holder for most arrests made as a NYPD detective, Madeline Wuntch quickly ascended through the ranks to Commissioner before her health no longer permitted her to work. Known for her decisive actions and incredible foresight, Ms. Wuntch lead the NYPD into an era of explosive growth and community acceptance before quietly retiring to her small cottage in Michigan due to her deteriorating health.

Ms. Wuntch was never married, nor had any children. Her personal life was intentionally kept quite private, except for her well-known friendship with NYPD captain Raymond Holt. Captain Holt collaborated with Ms. Wuntch several times over her stellar career, eventually playing a major role in securing her the role of Commissioner.

"The Derek Jeeter incident was purely the fault of Raymond Jacob Holt"

-Madeline Wuntch
